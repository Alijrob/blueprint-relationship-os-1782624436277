## Basic Information

- **Full name:** Maya Reyes
- **Preferred name or nickname:** Maya
- **Relationship to me:** Partner
- **Pronouns:** she/her
- **Location:** Portland, Oregon
- **How we met:** Introduced by mutual friends at a backyard barbecue in the summer of 2021,
  then kept running into each other at the same Saturday farmers market.
- **Roughly how long we have known each other:** About five years, partners for the last three.
- **Best ways to reach them:** Text for anything quick, a call in the evening for anything that
  needs real talk. She rarely checks email outside work hours.

## Preferences

What this person tends to enjoy or favor. Be specific.

- **Food and drink:** Loves dark chocolate and dislikes milk chocolate. Big fan of Thai and
  Vietnamese food, especially anything with fresh herbs and lime. Drinks oat milk lattes in the
  morning and peppermint tea at night. Prefers dry red wine over sweet.
- **Hobbies and interests:** Trail running, pottery (she has a standing Tuesday class), tending
  a balcony herb garden, and weekend thrifting for vintage denim.
- **Music, books, shows, games:** Indie folk and 90s hip hop, literary fiction and nature
  writing, cozy mystery shows on slow nights, and a long-running co-op habit of Stardew Valley.
- **Style and brands:** Practical and earthy. Likes Patagonia and Everlane, natural fabrics,
  and a small rotation of well-made pieces over fast fashion.
- **Favorite places:** The Oregon coast around Cannon Beach, the neighborhood pottery studio,
  and a quiet corner cafe on Hawthorne that does the oat latte right.
- **Other things they love:** Handwritten notes, fresh flowers (especially ranunculus), early
  morning quiet, and a well-organized weekend with one thing planned and the rest left open.

## Dislikes

What this person tends to avoid, dislike, or react poorly to. Specifics help the assistant
keep suggestions safe and on target.

- **Food and drink to avoid:** Milk chocolate, very sweet cocktails, and heavy cream sauces,
  which do not sit well with her. Not a fan of cilantro-heavy dishes despite loving other herbs.
- **Activities or settings they dislike:** Loud crowded bars, last-minute plans that scrap a
  quiet evening she was looking forward to, and long days with no time outdoors.
- **Topics they would rather not discuss:** Office politics after hours, and detailed talk
  about money in a group setting.
- **Pet peeves:** Being interrupted mid-thought, dishes left in the sink overnight, and people
  who are chronically late without a heads up.

## Gift Ideas

- **Things they have mentioned wanting:** A new pair of trail running shoes, a set of glazes
  for her pottery, and a weekend trip to the coast in the off season.
- **Sizes, colors, or specs that matter:** Shoe size US 8, womens. Clothing size medium.

- **Gifts to avoid:** Strong perfumes, anything milk chocolate, and gadgets that add screens to
  her downtime.
- **Budget sense for this person:** Values thoughtful over expensive. A well-chosen forty dollar
  gift lands better than a generic pricey one. Birthday and anniversary are where bigger spends
  feel right.

## Communication Style

How this person likes to communicate, so your timing and tone fit them.

- **Preferred channel:** Text for logistics, in person or a call for anything emotional.
- **Pace and timing:** Quick to reply during the day, goes quiet in the evening when she is
  winding down. Prefers a heads up before a serious conversation rather than being sprung on it.
- **Tone that works:** Warm and direct. She appreciates plain honesty delivered kindly and gets
  frustrated by hinting or hedging.
- **What to avoid in how you communicate:** Do not raise heavy topics late at night, and do not
  try to fix a problem before she has finished explaining it. She wants to be heard first.
